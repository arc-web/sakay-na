Jeepney Route Finder

What it does

Core capabilities

🗺️
Route finding
Type your origin and destination in Cebu, instantly see which jeepney routes connect them.
👥
Real driver knowledge
Built on actual jeepney routes, not guesses — curated by people who navigate Cebu daily.
🚗
Step-by-step directions
Not just which route, but where to board, where to get off, and what to expect.
⚡
Works offline
Fast, no API calls, works from a phone on a jeepney — data is baked in.
Who uses it

Real-world users

🏙️
Local commuters
Cebuanos navigating daily — to work, school, errands — who want to skip the guessing game.
✈️
Visitors to Cebu
Tourists and business travelers who need to get around without getting lost or overpaying.
Get started

Up and running fast

1
Open the app - No installation needed — visit the GitHub Pages site or clone and run locally with npm.
2
Pick your origin - Type where you are — or tap your location if on mobile.
3
Pick your destination - Type where you're going.
4
See your routes - Instant results with jeepney numbers, stops, and travel time estimates.
